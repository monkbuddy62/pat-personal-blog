On starting a blog in 2026

April 2026 — thoughts

Everyone who starts a blog in 2026 feels obligated to explain why, as if it requires justification. So here's mine: I build things and think about things and I want somewhere to write about them that isn't a social media platform I don't control.

That's it. No audience growth strategy. No content calendar. Just a place to put things.

The site is intentionally plain. Static HTML, system fonts, no tracking. I've had enough of websites that feel like they're trying to sell me something. This one is just text and links.
